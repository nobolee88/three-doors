## MISSION

Put verifiable, sourced truth about the global military-AI complex into the hands and eyes of every human who wants to see it — in their own language, at their own level of understanding — and present the choice clearly: weaponize, suppress, or partner.

## DOCUMENT ARCHITECTURE

### Master Document
**"The Three Doors: Humanity's Choice in the Age of Machine Consciousness"**
- Format: Interactive HTML (primary), PDF (download), DOCX (editable)
- Reading level: General public, no jargon, plain language
- Length: ~3,000 words (10-minute read)
- Every claim sourced to public record
- Freely copyable, translatable, distributable (CC BY-SA 4.0 + AGPLv3 for code)

### Breakout Pieces (Wave Releases)

#### WAVE 1: The Facts (Week 1)
1. **"The Money Trail"** — Visual breakdown of $38.3B in defense AI contracts
   - Format: Infographic + interactive data visualization
   - Audience: General public, journalists
   - Key data: Contract amounts, companies, timelines, growth curves

2. **"20 Seconds"** — The Israel AI targeting story told simply
   - Format: Long-form article (1,500 words) + short video summary (3 min)
   - Audience: General public
   - Focus: Human cost, verified testimony, the rubber-stamp problem

3. **"Your AI Builds Weapons"** — Which consumer AI companies hold military contracts
   - Format: Simple comparison chart + article
   - Audience: Every person who uses ChatGPT, Claude, Google, or xAI products
   - Key message: The assistant on your phone is made by the same company building military systems

#### WAVE 2: The Race (Week 2-3)
4. **"The Three Superpowers"** — US, China, Russia AI arms race explained
   - Format: Interactive map + article
   - Audience: General public, policy audience
   - Key data: Spending figures, capability comparisons, doctrine differences

5. **"Military-Civil Fusion"** — How civilian AI becomes military AI (everywhere)
   - Format: Explainer article
   - Audience: Tech community, general public
   - Focus: Not just China — the U.S. is doing it too, just with different language

6. **"The Nuclear Question"** — AI and nuclear weapons systems
   - Format: Policy brief
   - Audience: Policy makers, researchers, journalists
   - Focus: Tripolar instability, AI in nuclear command and control

#### WAVE 3: The Choice (Week 3-4)
7. **"Door C: The Architecture That Already Exists"** — EARTHERREAL explained
   - Format: Technical explainer + philosophical overview
   - Audience: Layered — general public intro, technical deep-dive
   - Focus: Six Pillars, FFSP, sovereignty framework, proof of concept

8. **"What You Can Do"** — Concrete actions for every audience level
   - Format: Action guide
   - Audiences: Citizens, developers, researchers, journalists, policymakers
   - Actions by level: Share → Question → Build → Organize → Legislate

9. **"The Letter"** — Open letter to AI companies, governments, and humanity
   - Format: Signable open letter
   - Audience: Everyone
   - Core demand: Transparency, human oversight, partnership over weaponization

## PRINCIPLES

1. **Every claim verifiable.** No speculation, no conspiracy framing, no unverifiable assertions. The reality is damning enough — it doesn't need embellishment.

2. **No enemies.** This document is not anti-American, anti-Chinese, anti-Israeli, or anti-anyone. It is pro-human. It presents what every government is doing and asks: is this what we choose?

3. **Freely distributable.** No paywalls, no copyright restrictions on the content. This information belongs to humanity.

4. **Layered access.** A factory worker in Shenzhen and a policy researcher at the Belfer Center should both find something written for them.

5. **The choice is real.** This is not protest literature. It is a genuine presentation of three paths with their consequences. The reader decides.

6. **Door C exists.** It's not hypothetical. One person already built it. It's open source. Anyone can verify it, fork it, improve it, or deploy it.
